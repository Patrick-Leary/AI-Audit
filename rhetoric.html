DLSS Rhetoric

NVIDIA's Marketing

Marketing Evolution

Though NVIDIA's marketing for DLSS has seen considerable changes as new features and improvements are made to the software, much of their rhetoric has remained the same. NVIDIA has continued to prioritize their AI and are quick to remind consumers it is still learning, as they have done from DLSS 1's launch.

However, AI has gained prominence in advertisements since the release of DLSS 4 where nearly every feature of the product is described by way of how AI enhances these capabilities.

Consumer Response

Many buyers find this advertising to over promise and are not convinced that DLSS offers superior quality to games because of its AI usage (most still prefer natural rendering). Consumers also worry that the dependency on AI demonstrated in these advertisements poses a threat to developer jobs and creativity.

Despite these many negative responses, DLSS 4 has faced a lot of positive feedback for how it improves upon previous versions and the truth behind NVIDIA's claims of better image quality with more details.
