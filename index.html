Understanding DLSS

What is DLSS?

DLSS allows games to render at lower resolutions to improve performance, then it upscales the image to enhance the image quality.

DLSS Super Resolution

  • Renders games at lower resolutions to boost performance
  • Uses AI to upscale to higher resolutions

DLSS Frame Generation

  • Creates entirely new frames between rendered ones
  • DLSS 3 generates one frame between each rendered frame
  • DLSS 4 can generate up to three frames per rendered frame

How It Works

NVIDIA trains DLSS on supercomputers by comparing its output against ultra-high quality 16K reference images thousands of times.

Advanced Features

  • Ray Reconstruction: Improves the quality of ray-traced scenes
  • Deep Learning Anti-Aliasing (DLAA): Uses DLSS technology to improve anti-aliasing at native resolution
