DLSS Super Resolution


AI-Powered Upscaling Technology

DLSS Super Resolution

DLSS Super Resolution uses AI to upscale lower resolution images to higher resolutions, improving performance at the cost of image quality

How It Works

Improved Rendering

  • Renders game at a lower resolution (e.g., 4k to 1080p)
  • Uses less GPU resources, resulting in higher frame rates

AI Upscaling

  • Runs AI upscaling model on GPU tensor cores
  • Compares current frame with motion data from previous frames
  • Reconstructs details to native resolution quality

Quality Options (Maybe include exact resolution scaling??)

  • Quality Mode: Highest image quality with moderate performance boost
  • Balanced Mode: Balance between quality and performance
  • Performance Mode: Large performance boost with some quality lost
  • Ultra Performance Mode: Extreme performance with significant quality loss and artifacting

Limitations

  • Image quality and artificating
  • DLSS varies by game

DLSS Frame Generation


Up to 3 Generated Frames for Every 1 Rendered Frame

DLSS Frame Generation

DLSS Frame Generation and Multi Frame Generation use AI to place generated frames in between rendered frames at the cost of latency

How It Works

Frame Generation Process

  • Analyzes previous frames to predict motion vectors
  • Generates entirely new frames between rendered frames
  • Significantly increases frame rate

Multi Frame Generation

  • Generates up to 3 frames between each rendered frame
  • Delivers up to 4x frame rate compared to no frame generation

Compatible Hardware

  • Multi Frame Gen: RTX 5000 series
  • 2x Frame Gen: RTX 4000 and 5000 series

Latency Considerations

  • Increases latency
